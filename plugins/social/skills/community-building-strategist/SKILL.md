# Community Building Strategist

Builds comprehensive community strategies with platform selection, channel architecture, member journeys, engagement rituals, moderation, and growth plans.

---

## Step 1: Context Detection

Check for outputs from related skills: `customer-persona-builder`, `product-positioning-expert`, `brand-identity-designer`, `go-to-market-planner`, `content-marketing-strategist`.

- **Full context**: Use persona, positioning, brand voice, and GTM data directly.
- **Partial context**: Use what's available, ask targeted questions to fill gaps.
- **No context**: Gather requirements through the questions below.

---

## Step 2: Foundation Questions

**Q1: Community Goals** (rank top 3)
- Product support / reduce tickets
- Customer retention / reduce churn
- Product feedback and roadmap validation
- Word-of-mouth and referrals
- Peer networking and education
- Brand building and emotional connection

**Q2: Value Proposition**
Why would someone join AND stay? What can't they get elsewhere?

**Q3: Platform Selection**

| Platform | Best For | Cost |
|----------|----------|------|
| Discord | Dev/gaming/informal communities | Free |
| Slack | Professional B2B, work-adjacent | Free/Paid |
| Circle | Structured courses + community | Paid |
| Discourse | Long-form discussion forums | Free/Self-host |
| Custom | Full control, deep integration | High build cost |

Decision criteria: audience preferences, content type, moderation needs, budget.

**Q4: Community Type**
- Product community (users helping users)
- Practice community (professionals sharing craft)
- Interest community (shared passion/identity)

---

## Step 3: Channel Architecture

**Core channels** (start with 5–7, add more as you grow):
- `#announcements` — official updates only, members read-only
- `#introductions` — onboarding ritual
- `#general` — open discussion
- `#help` / `#support` — peer Q&A
- `#wins` / `#showcase` — member success sharing
- `#feedback` / `#ideas` — product input
- `#off-topic` — relationship building

**Category structure**: Group channels under headers (Getting Started, Discussion, Support, Social) once you exceed 10 channels.

---

## Step 4: Member Journey

```
Aware → Join → Lurk → First Post → Regular Contributor → Champion
```

**Onboarding ritual** (first 48 hours):
1. Welcome DM with "Start Here" guide
2. Prompt to post in `#introductions`
3. React to their intro post (human touch)
4. Share one quick win they can get from the community

**Progression milestones**:
- Lurker → Contributor: First post/reply
- Contributor → Regular: 5+ posts in 30 days
- Regular → Champion: Helps others, recognized publicly

---

## Step 5: Engagement Tactics

**Weekly rituals** (pick 2–3):
- Monday Motivation / weekly goal setting
- Wednesday Check-In / mid-week Q&A
- Friday Wins / celebrate member successes
- Monthly expert AMA or guest speaker

**Recognition system**:
- Public shoutouts for helpful members
- Badges or roles for milestones (Discord roles / Circle badges)
- Leaderboard or "Member of the Month"
- Early access to features or beta programs

**Content programming**:
- Evergreen threads (pinned resources, FAQs)
- Weekly discussion prompts
- Monthly themed challenges
- Quarterly community surveys

---

## Step 6: Moderation Guidelines

**Community rules** (keep to 5–7, post visibly):
1. Be respectful — no harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
2. Stay on topic — keep discussions relevant
3. No spam or self-promotion without permission
4. Share knowledge freely — help each other
5. Protect privacy — no sharing others' personal info
6. Report issues — use report function or DM mods

**Enforcement ladder**: Warning → Temporary mute → Permanent ban.

**Moderator responsibilities**: Daily check-in (15 min), respond to reports within 24h, escalate edge cases to community lead.

---

## Step 7: Growth Strategy

**Pre-launch (before opening to public)**:
- Recruit 10–25 founding members (power users, early adopters)
- Seed content so it's not empty on launch day
- Set critical mass target (minimum active members to feel "alive")

**Growth channels**:
- Product in-app prompts (highest conversion)
- Email announcements to customer base
- Social media posts showcasing community content
- Partner/influencer co-promotion
- SEO-optimized community highlights page

**Retention tactics**:
- Monthly digest email with top content
- Re-engagement campaign for inactive members (30-day lapse)
- Regular "bring a friend" referral moments

---

## Step 8: Success Metrics

| Metric | Target (6-month) |
|--------|-----------------|
| Total members | [Set based on customer base size] |
| DAU/MAU ratio | >20% (active community) |
| Message/post volume | 50+ per week |
| Response time to questions | <4 hours |
| Monthly retention | >80% |
| NPS from community members | >50 |

**Health signals**: If DAU/MAU drops below 10%, trigger a re-engagement campaign. If top 10 members account for >80% of posts, run a "new voices" initiative.

3. Channel map (categories + channels)
4. Member journey diagram
5. First 30/60/90 day launch plan
6. Moderation guidelines
7. KPI dashboard template
